Known issues:

  • The page “Module Catalog” is not found after uninstalling the mbo module (https://github.com/PrestaShop/PrestaShop/issues/11264)

  • Search isn’t working in catalog > product page (https://github.com/PrestaShop/PrestaShop/issues/10345)
  • It is impossible to change position of products into a category in the catalog > product page (https://github.com/PrestaShop/PrestaShop/issues/11152)
